Join our Women's Pilates classes at the Malikah Center! This class is led by an instructor in a supportive and inclusive environment where you can feel comfortable and confident in exploring the world of Pilates. Please bring a personal yoga mat to the class.


Pilates emphasizes core strength, alignment, and breath control, with exercises designed to target specific muscle groups while maintaining proper form and alignment. It can be tailored to individual fitness levels and goals, making it suitable for people of all ages and abilities. Pilates is known for its benefits in improving posture, increasing flexibility, enhancing muscular strength and endurance, and promoting relaxation and stress relief.

EID-AL-ADHA Picnic

DATE: 06/19/24
TIME: 2:00 pm - 6:00 pm
LOCATION: CENTRAL PARK

Join us for a joyful Eid-al-Adha picnic celebration at Central Park! Gather with friends, family, and fellow community members for a day of festivities and relaxation in the heart of nature. Indulge in delicious homemade treats and traditional Eid delicacies as we mark this special occasion together. Take part in fun outdoor activities such as games, storytelling, and group prayers amidst the serene surroundings of Central Park. With laughter, joy, and camaraderie, let's create cherished memories and celebrate the spirit of Eid-al-Adha in the company of loved ones. Don't forget to bring your picnic blankets, smiles, and festive spirit!

DATE: 06/05/24
